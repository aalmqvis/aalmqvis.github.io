Education

2019

MScEng (Civilingengörsexamen) in Media Technology | KTH Royal Institute of Technology | Stockholm, Sweden
My thesis Sites of Encounter explores challenges of situated alienation from people and place in my neighbourhood. With it, I address a need for particular social experiences, which, besides the sake of social wellbeing, it is contributing to a valuable foundation for local, collective and coordinated action. I propose four design instances and reflections designing with people where they live.
